About Us

Who We Are

Wild Eden is a professional company providing high quality gardening services. Founded and managed by Nick Keepen, after he graduated with a degree in horticulture, Wild Eden is a team of gardeners dedicated to providing a friendly, reliable, service at an affordable price.

What We Do

We offer garden maintenance packages of outstanding quality in Surrey to suit all garden sizes and budgets, from a quick trim and tidy to a full garden design and makeover.

With our gardening knowledge and experience, you can be sure to receive sound, up-to-date, professional advice on all aspects of gardening.

This coupled with superb customer service standards, has allowed us to build our business on personal recommendations.
