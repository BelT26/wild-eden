Who We Are

About Nick Keepen BSc (Hons) Hort, MCI Hort

I graduated from Writtle College, Chelmsford in 2000 with my degree in Horticulture. I worked for a landscaping company in London for some time but soon wanted to create my own business and Horticultural Services was born in 2003 providing its services in London and Surrey

Organic growth of customers and reputation led to employing a small, but dynamic team of professional, dedicated staff.

My team and I now provide a friendly, efficient service of on-going garden maintenance and advice to an established clientele of satisfied customers in and around Dorking in Surrey.

What We Do

We provide garden maintenance packages of outstanding quality in the Dorking area to suit all garden sizes and budgets, from a quick trim and tidy to a full garden clearance, and everything in between! View the Gallery page for beautiful before/after pictures.

We pride ourselves in offering a friendly, reliable, value for money service to our customers, many of whom have been benefitting from our unique style for over 15 years.
