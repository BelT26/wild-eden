FAQs

Where are you based and what areas do you cover?

We are based in Dorking and provide Gardening Services to homeowners and commercial properties throughout East Surrey and the surrounding area.

Contact us to find out if we cover your area.

Will I have to pay for a consultation or quote?

No. Simply contact us and we will arrange to visit you at a convenient time to discuss your needs and prepare a free, no obligation quote.

How do I make payment?

When we agree the gardening services you require and the regularity of our visits we will ask you to set up a monthly standing order. You will receive a monthly invoice and payments can be adjusted as needed.

Do you supply garden materials and flowers?

We will discuss and agree with you first any materials required. As we have relationships with local suppliers we will then source these on your behalf and add the cost to your invoice.

Do you take away all the generated waste?

We are registered waste carriers with the Environment Agency and can take any waste away for a charge. This will then be disposed of properly at a local waste transfer station.
